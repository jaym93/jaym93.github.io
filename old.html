Interfaces will disappear, yet work to help you fulfill your objective. And I want to be right in the middle of where it all happens.

Data-driven Smart Home


Aggregation of data streams from sensors for intent discovery, smart learning and ambient alerting for the future connected home.
Highly modular approach, standalone services and open API for OEM support are planned. Project is in early stages of development, quickly picking up pace. Support for voice and text interface are under progress.

CS8903 Special Problems for the Aware Home Research Initiative, Georgia Tech

Role: Envisioning, System architecture, protocol architecture, Prototyping, NLP and conversational UI design, documentation and preperation of training material


SociaLight

CS8803 Intro to HCI, Georgia Tech | Team of 4

Role: Contextual study, product design and development, UI and backend development, documentation

MiniCity ATL: GoodieX Hackathon


A hackathon against Gentrification in metropolitan cities, GoodieNation helps under-served communities with support from volunteers to create solutions with technology and social impact to spread awareness.
For the publicity and marketing hackathon, we had to create branding guidelines, mission and vision, website and other marketing materials for a start-up called MiniCity ATL.

Publicity and marketing, 48 hour Hackathon | Team of 10

Role: Web design, backend integrations consulting, brand guide and graphics design

Internet controlled switchboard


Controlling groups of electrical appliances with relays and Raspberry Pi, incorporating learning and monitoring patterns of usage in college buildings. Won the Infosys Project Innovation Award.
The project was only shown off with a very basic HTML UI, but the potential of application was large. This project got me interested in IoT and design, and was my first project with Raspberry Pi.

Undergrad Capstone project, NIE Institute of Technology. | Team of 4

Role: Circuit design, Raspberry Pi scripting, Server configuration


SLA Report Generator


One of my first attempts at automation of workflows, this tool connected to the database and showed a dashboard with status and a graphical view, and provided the functionality to generate reports that were manually generated in Excel thus far.
Though not the best project in terms of UI design, this project got me started on observing what users do regularly that could be taken over by simple scripts.

Automation and process streamlining, Hewlett Packard Enterprise | Team of 2

Role: Ethnography, UI wireframing, Web design, Servlet integration, Testing


Logo Design and branding for Aware Home


Graphics and brand guidelines created for the Data-Driven Smart Home and Smart Home Dashboard projects

Aware Home Research Initiative, Georgia Institute of Technology | Individual

Role: Iconography, graphics design and brand guidelines creation


Timesheet Compliance tool


An intrusive Windows Forms application that won't let you proceed till you say you've filled your timesheet. (Avoid billing losses at all costs, if you will excuse the pun.)
Eventually, it evolved to support server-side reminders and automated data gathering from SAP systems, from just triggering based on time conditions.

Automation and process streamlining, Hewlett Packard Enterprise | Team of 4

Role: Application conceptualization, Wireframing, Windows Forms design, Web UI design, Trigger algorithm optimization, server-side SAP interface scripting, documentation and presentation
